One Piece Film Red (Light Novel) (One Shot)

Book description: "One Piece" 25th anniversary work! Her singing voice, which hides her identity, is described as "another dimension". Such Uta will appear in public for the first time at a live performance! While fans from all over the world, including Luffy's Straw Hat Pirates, pirates, navy, and all sorts of fans who have come to enjoy her singing voice, fill the venue, Uta is called "Shanks' daughter". Luffy challenges the world-shaking battle! Eiichiro Oda, the original author, serves as the general producer. Directed by Goro Taniguchi, who has received high acclaim both in Japan and overseas for his work on the popular anime "Code Geass" series. Released on August 6, 2022, the fastest novelization of the long-awaited latest movie version for the first time in three years!
